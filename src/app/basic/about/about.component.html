History

M.B.Patel Science College
was established in 1967 , and has total Campus area of 28852.38 Sq. mtrs. and the total built- up area is 7542.74 Sq.mtr. Started with subjects Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology. It is affiliated to Sardar Patel University , Vallabh Vidyanagar.
The college is recognized under section 2(f) and 12 B of the UGC Act 1956. The College is Reaccredited by NAAC with a CGPA of 3.02 at ‘A’ Grade in October 2013. The College is Accredited by KCG for ‘AAA’ with a 3.11 CGPA at A-Grade in January 2015.
The College was awarded “College with Potential for Excellence” status by University Grants Commission, New Delhi in 2011and received a grant of One Crore under this Scheme.
The College is selected “College with Potential for Excellence” by University Grants Commission, New Delhi for the Second phase of five year from 1st April 2017 to 31st March 2022 and is sanctioned grant of Rs. One Crore Thirty Lacks under this Scheme.

In 1967 the college offered – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology and English and as per the demand of the society, the other subjects were introduced :

  • 1979 – Statistics
  • 1980 – Microbiology
  • 1981 – Biochemistry
  • 1985 – Electronics & Computer Science
  • 2000 – BCA & DMLT
  • 2004 – Biotechnology (SF)
  • 2005 – M.Sc. (Organic Chemistry)
  • 2006 – M.Sc. (Microbiology)
  • 2008 – M.Sc. (Biotechnology)
  • 2008 – M.Sc. (Medical Laboratory Technology)
  • 2009 – Ph.D. Center in Chemistry
  • 2010 – Ph.D. Center in Biochemistry in Food & Nutrition.
  • 2018 – Zoology, Botany & Industrial Chemistry

For administrative convenience all the P.G. courses were separated from M.B.Patel Science College and started functioning under a separate P.G.Institute namely Shri A.N.Patel P.G.Institute for Science and Research in 2010. The BCA course was also shifted from M.B.Patel Science College and started under a separate Institute namely Shri D.N.Institute of Computer Applications in 2012.

Activities:

The College is authorized centre for SCOPE and DELL, initiatives of Government of Gujarat Since 2008, The College is also recognized as a Exam Centre for same. Every year more than one thousand students appear for SCOPE ‘A’ and ‘B’ Level Exams.
  • The College has Boys NCC Unit which is affiliated with 4 Gujarat Battalion NCC,Vallabh Vidyanagar Group, Gujarat.
  • The College has Girls NCC Unit and is affiliated with 4 Gujarat Girls Battalion,Vallabh Vidyanagar Group, Gujarat.
  • The College has NSS Unit. The NSS Unit carries out a number of activities in the College like Tree plantation, Blood Donation Camp, “Swatchta Abhiyan” etc. Every year the NSS Unit organizes Annual Camp of Seven days at near by Village and carry out a number of activities like Gram Survey, Medical Check up Camps and Community Awareness programs in the Village.
  • The students of the College participate in Inter College University Sports Events. The Students of the College are selected in Sardar Patel University Team in Various Sports.
  • In 2014 the Girls Table-Tennis and Volley ball Team became champions at University level and the Boys Hand ball Team became Runners Up at University level .
  • In 2015 the Girls Table-Tennis and Volley ball Team became champions at University level and the Boys Cricket Team became Runners Up at University level .
  • In 2016 Boys Kabaddi Team became Runners Up at University level.
  • In 2017 Kishan Ankoliya student of the college was selected as the Captain of the Sardar Patel University Cricket Team.
  • The Students of the College also participate in the cultural activities organized by the College and Youth Festival organized by the University and win prizes at the Youth Festival.
  • In 2015 students of the college were selected to represent Sardar Patel University at the 31st Inter University Yuvi West Fest West Zone organized by Association of Indian Universities.
  • In 2017 students of the college participated in the National Competition in Dance and Drama held at Solan,Himachal Pradesh Abhinay-2017 and they achieved First Position in One Act Play, over All Best Performance, Best Director, Best Actor and Best Lighting.
  • The College arranges Study Tours every year to give the students practical exposure.
  • The College arranges Campus Interviews for the Final year students.
  • The Departments of the College every year arrange Guest lectures by experts from Industry/Institutions/PG departments for Staff & Students.
  • The College felicitates the Students who excel in academics, sports,NCC & NSS .The Annual day celebrations by giving the trophies & Medals and certificate of appreciation.
  • The College felicitates the Staff members who publish research papers, books and are awarded Ph.D. degree at the Annual Day celebrations by giving them Medals and certificate of appreciation.
  • The College gives a cash prize of Rs.2500/- to the Department whose students awarded Gold Medal at University Examination.
